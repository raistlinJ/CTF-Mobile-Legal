## What to include in your message

To help diagnose issues quickly, please include:

- Your iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.x)
- Your device model (or simulator)
- The CTFd version (if known)
- The CTFd base URL you configured (you can redact private hostnames)
- Steps to reproduce
- Any error message shown in the app
